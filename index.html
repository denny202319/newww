Our acquisition of Rocki is a giant leap into the future of blockchain-powered music.

It’s an exciting time for us at Curios. As pioneers in the blockchain, NFT, and web3 spaces, we’re always looking for ways to empower digital content creators and musicians. Today, we’re thrilled to share that we’ve entered into an agreement to acquire Rocki, a groundbreaking venture initiated by the legendary founders of CryptoPunks, Matt Hall and John Watkinson, alongside music industry stalwarts Gareth Emery and Bjorn Niclas.

Our blockchain-based approach is not just a new business model, but a new philosophy for music streaming and artist empowerment.
